fun <T> Flow<T>.produceIn(scope: CoroutineScope): ReceiveChannel<T>
Content copied to clipboard

Launch a job in the scope collecting this flow and sending the values to a channel, which is returned.

The channel is closed when the flow completes. On failure, the scope is not cancelled, instead the channel is closed with the error.
